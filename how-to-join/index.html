Becoming a Member

Please note that membership sign up is not available until the start of the academic year.

To join:

Visit the SU Website and follow the instructions to join our physics family! Becoming a member will give you access to all of our socials, talks, and trips.

For more information, have a look at our physics freshers Facebook Page.

To join our mailing list and to receive weekly updates on events, email majordomo@durham.ac.uk with the body: subscribe ds-physsoc

This is the only way to be sure you don't miss out on anything!
